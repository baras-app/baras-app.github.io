## Quick Preview

**Q: What operating systems does BARAS support?**
A: Windows, macOS, and Linux.

**Q: Do I need to enable combat logging in SWTOR?**
A: Yes, combat logging must be enabled in your SWTOR settings.

**Q: Is BARAS safe to use?**
A: BARAS only reads combat log files and doesn't interact with the game client.
